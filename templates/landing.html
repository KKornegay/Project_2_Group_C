Year: Wins Vs. Cost Per Win

The data set included with this exercise depicts relationships between wins and cost in the NBL by year. Additional information can be obtained including: Championship status, Cost per win, Team salary, Ave player salary, and number of wins relative to a specific year.

Team: Year vs Cost Per Win.

The data set included with this exercise depicts time vs cost/win in NBL bassed on a team of choice. Each datapoint corresponds to the number of wins for a NBL team with additional information available. Data includes: Team name, Championship status, Cost per win, Team salary, Ave player salary, and the year which the data was extracted. Green indicates world champion.
